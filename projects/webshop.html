Webshop for local grocery store

Purpose

When I just started with my student-business, I made a call to a local grocery store to ask them if they needed a website (they didn't have one at the moment). They were interested and asked me if I could make a small webshop system without payment functionality to decrease the amount of phone orders they needed to process.

Development process

I had never done such a thing before so I started by researching the options. There was no time pressure on the project and I wanted to learn as much as I could, so I handcoded the website with PHP as backend language. I also made a small CMS for the owners so they can update their product information. I have learned a lot of things about website development during this project. Mainly that I don't really like PHP and that I will use ASP.NET for the backend whenever I can.
